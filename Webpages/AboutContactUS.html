About Us

Welcome to Eye Candy Cinemas.
Eye Candy Cinemas are edgy, alternative venues catering to horror film buffs and lovers of cinema.
We offer regular screenings of classic films, cult b-movies, and the latest releases in horror. The experiences we create are unique and both a great start to a night out and a main event – our theatre seats are super plush, with free seating in the boutique screening rooms, and we are fully licensed with a curated selection of local wine, craft beer, and a selection of classic movie snacks.
We have two locations across New Zealand, one in Kingsland and another in Newlands.
